Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 30, 2025

jayBird Projects ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our GitHub App.

Information We Collect

When you install jayBird Projects, we collect:

  • GitHub Installation Data: Your GitHub organization or user account name, installation ID, and the repositories you grant access to.
  • Project Data: Issue titles, descriptions, dates, estimates, dependencies, and milestone information from GitHub Projects you configure with our app.
  • Billing Information: If you subscribe to our paid plan, payment processing is handled by Stripe. We store your Stripe customer ID but never store credit card numbers.

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Calculate and update dates on your GitHub Project items based on dependencies and estimates
  • Track baseline schedules and generate variance reports
  • Process subscription payments and manage your account
  • Respond to support requests
  • Improve our service

Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely on Railway's infrastructure. We implement industry-standard security measures including:

  • Encrypted connections (HTTPS) for all data transmission
  • Webhook signature verification for all GitHub events
  • Secure environment variable storage for credentials

Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as you have jayBird Projects installed. When you uninstall the app, we delete your installation data within 30 days. Audit logs may be retained for up to 90 days for security purposes.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • GitHub: For authentication and accessing your project data
  • Stripe: For payment processing
  • Railway: For hosting our application

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the data we hold about your installation
  • Request deletion of your data by uninstalling the app
  • Export your data upon request

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at privacy@jaybird.us or open an issue on our GitHub repository.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.
